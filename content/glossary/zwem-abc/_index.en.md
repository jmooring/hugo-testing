Dutch national swimming skills certification program, which includes unified exams and diplomas.

It recognises the following three degrees: **A**, **B**, and **ะก**. Hence the name {{< fl "Zwem-ABC" >}} = {{< fl "zwemmen" >}} (swim) + {{< fl "ABC" >}}.
